These Bases Will Be Hurt First By Climate Change

Rising Seas Will Damage US Bases

BY Kate Sliney

Published December 10, 2018

Sea level rise threatens to engulf military installations along the US Coastline. Four U.S. Military installations risk losing 75% - 90% of their land.

1. Naval Air Station Key West

The Naval Air Station at Key West will be one of the most dramatically affected U.S. military installations. Just 3 feet in sea level rise will put 87% of the base underwater. Scientists predict the world's ocean may rise by 3ft by the end of the century.
